Welcome to HabitTrack

You Are Ready to Build Your First Routine

Start simple and let the app grow with you. A good first habit should feel clear, small, and easy to repeat.

1. Add one small habit

Think tiny: drink water, read one page, or stretch for two minutes.

2. Use low-effort days when needed

You can protect your streak without forcing a full-energy version every time.

3. Explore charts and suggestions later

Once you log a few days, HabitTrack will start surfacing patterns and next-step ideas.
